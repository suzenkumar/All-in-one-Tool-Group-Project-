# Dynamic programming approach — O(n*m) time complexity def lcs(s1: str, s2: str) -> str: m, n = len(s1), len(s2) dp = [[0] * (n + 1) for _ in range(m + 1)] for i in range(1, m + 1): for j in range(1, n + 1): if s1[i-1] == s2[j-1]: dp[i][j] = dp[i-1][j-1] + 1 else: dp[i][j] = max(dp[i-1][j], dp[i][j-1]) result = [] while m > 0 and n > 0: if s1[m-1] == s2[n-1]: result.append(s1[m-1]); m -= 1; n -= 1 elif dp[m-1][n] > dp[m][n-1]: m -= 1 else: n -= 1 return ''.join(reversed(result))
"Solve: ∫ x² · e^x dx using integration by parts."
Using Integration by Parts: ∫u·dv = u·v − ∫v·du
Step 1: Let u = x², dv = eˣ dx → du = 2x dx, v = eˣ
Step 2: Apply IBP: x²eˣ − 2∫xeˣ dx
Step 3: Second IBP: let u = x → xeˣ − ∫eˣ dx = xeˣ − eˣ
Step 4: x²eˣ − 2(xeˣ − eˣ)
✅ Final Answer: eˣ(x² − 2x + 2) + C

❓ FAQ

Common Questions Answered

How do I get started with StudentAI Tools?

Sign up for free — no credit card required. You'll instantly get access to our free tier with 10 tool uses per day. Students with a .edu or .ac email can verify for 50% off the Pro plan automatically.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption on all stored data, follow GDPR regulations, and operate on a zero-knowledge architecture. Your academic documents are never used to train any AI models and are auto-deleted after 30 days unless saved.

Will AI-assisted work violate academic integrity policies?

Our tools are designed as learning aids, not shortcuts. We encourage using our Essay Generator for outlines and structure, our Code Assistant to understand logic, and our Math Solver to learn from step-by-step solutions. Always follow your institution's guidelines.

How does the student discount verification work?

Sign up with your .edu or .ac.uk email address. We automatically verify your student status and apply the 50% discount instantly. If your institution doesn't use these domains, you can also upload a student ID photo for manual verification within 24 hours.

Can I collaborate with classmates on group projects?

Yes! The Team Plan (from $19.99/month for 5 users) enables real-time document collaboration, shared flashcard decks, group quizzes, shared research libraries, and a group leaderboard. You can see who's editing what in real time, leave comments, and maintain full version history.

What subjects and languages are supported?

We support every major academic subject — STEM, humanities, business, law, medicine, arts, and more. Our language tools support 100+ languages for translation, grammar checking, and content generation. The Code Assistant supports 50+ programming languages.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Native iOS and Android apps are available for free download. They sync seamlessly with your web account and are fully featured, including the Pomodoro timer, flashcard review mode, and AI chat assistant — perfect for studying on the go.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime with one click from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, no contracts, and no tricks. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period, and we'll send you an export of all your saved work before deactivating.
